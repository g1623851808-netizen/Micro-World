在 Minecraft 中探索真正的宇宙！建造火箭进入宇宙维度，在深蓝色的星空中发现 300 个彩色光点，每个光点对应一个独特的世界——生命世界、矿物世界、岩浆世界、末影世界等 12 种类型。穿上宇航服在宇宙中飞行，用星门秘钥在不同星球间穿梭，使用星球定位器搜索附近的光点。地狱和末地已被封印，所有独有物资都分布在对应的星球世界中。

Explore a real universe in Minecraft! Build rockets to enter the cosmos dimension, discover 300 colorful light points in the deep blue starry sky. Each light point leads to a unique world — Lush, Mineral, Desert, Ocean, Lava, End, Frozen, Shadow, Dirt, Sky, and Mixed types. Wear spacesuits to fly in space, use the Stargate Key to travel between planets, and use the Planet Locator to search nearby light points. The Nether and End are sealed, all unique resources are distributed in the corresponding planet worlds.

🌌 宇宙维度：纯虚空、缓降机制、深蓝星空背景、星云与动态星星

🪐 300 个彩色光点：12 种颜色对应不同星球类型，圆形发光，256 格内可见

🚀 火箭：一次性物品，右键传送到宇宙（主世界/星球世界均可使用）

🔑 星门秘钥：暗能量系统（上限 50，恢复 1/分钟），跨维度传送，传送消耗 10 点

👨‍🚀 宇航服四件套：头盔、胸甲、护腿、靴子，拥有飞行、护甲+10、速度、免疫宇宙伤害等功能，燃料管理（100 上限，每 60 秒消耗 1 点，手持燃料右键补充）

🛠️ 太空建造器：手持右键在宇宙中放置石块，方便搭建空间站

💨 氧气生成器：提供 32 格半径安全区域，范围内不需要宇航服

📡 星球定位器：手持右键搜索直径 9000 格内的光点，显示类型、方向、距离、坐标（消耗 50 暗能量）

🌍 12 种星球世界：有限半径 1000~3000 格，每种世界有独特的地形和资源（地狱/末地物资均在对应星球）

🚫 地狱/末地禁用：传送门无法激活，所有资源必须通过星球世界获取

🛡️ 宇航服穿戴优化：右键自动穿戴到空位，手持燃料右键补充

📋 游戏内帮助：/microworld 指令查看所有指令列表

Features
🌌 Cosmos Dimension – Pure void with slow descent, deep blue starry background, nebulae, and dynamic twinkling stars

🪐 300 Colored Light Points – 12 colors corresponding to different planet types, glowing circles, visible within 256 blocks

🚀 Rocket – One-time use item, right-click to teleport to the cosmos (works from both Overworld and planet worlds)

🔑 Stargate Key – Dark energy system (max 50, recovers 1/min), cross-dimension teleportation, costs 10 energy per use

👨‍🚀 Spacesuit Set (4 pieces) – Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots. Grants flight, +10 armor, speed boost, and immunity to cosmic damage. Fuel system: max 100, consumes 1 every 60 seconds, refill by right-clicking with Astro Fuel

🛠️ Space Builder – Right-click to place a stone block in front of you in the cosmos, perfect for building space stations

💨 Oxygen Generator – Creates a safe zone within a 32-block radius, no spacesuit needed inside the area

📡 Planet Locator – Right-click to scan light points within a 9000-block diameter, displays type, direction, distance, and coordinates (costs 50 dark energy)

🌍 12 Planet World Types – Limited radius of 1000–3000 blocks, each with unique terrain and resources (Nether/End materials found in corresponding planets)

🚫 Nether & End Disabled – Portals cannot be activated, all resources must be obtained through planet worlds

🛡️ Smart Spacesuit Equip – Right-click to auto-equip to empty armor slots, right-click with fuel to refill

📋 In-Game Help – Use /microworld command to view all available commands
